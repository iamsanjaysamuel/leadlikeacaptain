About the Author

Manager. Recruiter.
Now Author.

Sanjay Samuel is a Talent Acquisition Leader at Uber, leading APAC tech and non-tech hiring across India and Japan. With over 14 years in HR and people leadership, he has built teams from scratch, scaled hiring functions across cultures and continents, and coached managers at every level of the journey.

His career began at a startup with no HR function and no team — where he built everything from the ground up, growing the company from 0 to 50 people. That experience shaped a core belief: great hiring and great leadership are both about people, not process.

After Amazon and 6+ years at Uber, he has led cross-market teams spanning Hyderabad, Bangalore, and Tokyo — and partnered with business leaders across India, Japan, and the US to align talent strategy with long-term growth. He leads with three beliefs: clarity in direction, coaching with intent, and service-driven leadership.

"I did not write this book because I have all the answers. I wrote it because I have been on this pitch long enough to share what has helped me — the good days, the tough ones, the wins, and the dropped catches."

Outside of work, Sanjay is a father of two, a cricket enthusiast, and someone who believes the best conversations happen over a strong cup of chai. Lead Like a Captain is his first book — written for every new manager figuring it out one day at a time.
